Step 1: Google Gemini Analysis

The Prompt:

I need to understand the psychographics of people who [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]. What are their: Core values and beliefs Emotional triggers and pain points Lifestyle aspirations Decision-making patterns Information consumption habits Social identity and self-image

Example: Yoga Studio Customers

🧘‍♀️ Mind-body wellness
🌱 Holistic health
⚖️ Work-life balance
🔄 Self-improvement
🌿 Natural remedies
🧠 Stress reduction
👥 Community connection
🛌 Sleep quality
📱 Wellness apps
🏃‍♂️ Active lifestyle

Key Insights Generated:

  • • Values: Wellness, balance, authenticity, mindfulness
  • • Pain Points: Stress, time constraints, feeling disconnected
  • • Aspirations: Inner peace, physical flexibility, community
  • • Decision Drivers: Instructor credentials, studio atmosphere, schedule flexibility
⬇
C

Step 2: Claude AI Persona Creation

The Prompt:

Based on these psychographic insights about [TARGET AUDIENCE], create 3 detailed customer personas. For each persona, include: Name, age, occupation Key values and beliefs Daily routines and habits Pain points and challenges Goals and aspirations Decision-making process Preferred communication channels Objections to overcome Ideal messaging approach

Example: Yoga Studio Persona

👩‍💼

Sarah, 34

Marketing Manager

Values: Work-life balance, wellness, authenticity

Daily Routine: Early riser, morning meditation, busy workday, evening yoga

Pain Points: Work stress, limited time, difficulty disconnecting

Decision Drivers: Studio proximity to work, class variety, instructor expertise

Messaging That Resonates: "Find your center in the chaos - our flexible schedule fits your busy life"

How This Transforms Your Marketing:

✓ Targeted messaging that speaks directly to emotional needs
✓ Content that addresses specific pain points
✓ Product positioning aligned with core values
✓ Objection handling tailored to each persona
